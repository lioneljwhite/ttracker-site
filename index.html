Privacy Policy

TTracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Overview

TTracker is designed to be private by default. The app is used to track testosterone therapy information such as injections, symptoms, lab results, reminders, and personal settings.

TTracker does not require account creation and does not collect, sell, or share personal data with the developer or third parties.

Data Collection

TTracker does not collect personal data from you.

  • No account is required to use the app.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs are used.
  • No health data, injection data, lab data, or symptom data is transmitted to the developer.

How Your Data Is Stored

  • By default, your data is stored locally on your device.
  • If you choose to enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in your private Apple iCloud account using Apple services.
  • The developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

Exported Backups

TTracker allows you to export your data for backup or transfer purposes. Exported files are created only when you choose to do so.

You are responsible for how exported files are stored, shared, or protected after export.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, TTracker may create local notifications on your device. Notification content is generated on-device and is not sent to the developer.

Health and Medical Information

TTracker may store information you choose to enter that relates to hormones, symptoms, lab values, and treatment history. This information remains on your device unless you choose to enable iCloud Sync or export it.

TTracker is provided for informational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Children’s Privacy

TTracker is intended for older teens and adults and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
